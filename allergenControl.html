Allergen Control

Product recalls due to allergens are on the rise! In the UK there were 110 food recalls specifically related to allergy risks in 2017/18, up 31% from 84 in the previous year.

The rules governing the control of allergens in food businesses are outlined in REGULATION (EU) No 1169/2011 on the provision of food information to consumers (EU FIC).

Businesses must inform their customer’s if any of the food products they produce contain any of the main 14 allergens as an ingredient.

This requires food businesses to:

    Provide allergen information to the consumer for both pre-packed and non-prepacked food or drink.

    Handle and manage food allergens adequately

    Ensure that staff are adequately trained in allergen awareness.

New legislation regarding the information which must be provided to consumer’s regarding foods which are pre-packed for direct sale (PPDS) is scheduled to be introduced in 2021. This will mandate full ingredients labelling for foods that have been produced and packed on the same premises from which they are being sold.

NFS can:

  • Provide expert advice and guidance on allergen control & management within your business and measures to mitigate any identified risks.
  • Verify your product labels against the allergen requirements as outlined in the EU FIR Regulation.
  • Provide allergen awareness training for your employees.
  • Conduct comprehensive allergen risk assessments of your processes.
  • Conduct a gap analysis against the requirements of the new PPDS legislation due to be introduced in 2021 and ensure that your business is compliant.

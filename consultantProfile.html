Food Safety Specialist

Tony Crilly BSc. (Hons) FIFST

Since graduating from Queen’s University Belfast with a BSc. (Hons) 2.1 in Food Science Tony has held a number of senior technical roles from Quality Systems Manager to Group Technical Manager in several food manufacturing businesses throughout the UK ranging in size from SME to large multi-national.

In a career spanning over 20 years in the industry, he has acquired extensive food technical management experience in numerous product categories including fresh & frozen meat, meat products & preparations, ambient, vegetarian, gluten free, dairy, bakery, high risk/ready to eat (RTE), and multi-component supplying both major retail and food service customers throughout the UK and Ireland.
